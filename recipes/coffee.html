How to make instant coffee

Ingredients for instant coffee:

  • Instant coffee of your choice
  • water
  • Optional: Sweetener, or milk if you prefer
  1. Boil water
  2. Pour in desired amount of instant coffee relative to cup size, and preferred strenght of coffee
  3. Pour the boiled water into the cup
  4. Mix the contents in the cup before adding your sweetener or milk
