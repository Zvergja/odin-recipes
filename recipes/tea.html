How to make tea

Tea is a wonderful drink that helps me relax when I code. Sometimes we all need a break from coffee!

To make tea easily all we need is:

  • Tea bag
  • Hot water
  • Sweetener of your choice (sugar or similar)

We can make tea in these few steps!

  1. Boil some water
  2. Put a teabag in a cup with your sweetener of choice
  3. Pour the hot water into the cup
  4. Wait for it to cool down a bit, and voila! You've got yourself a wonderful cup of tea! Enjoy
